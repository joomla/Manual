Joomla 5.4.3 Known Issues
=========================

Last Updated: 28 February 2026

[Joomla 5.4.3](https://github.com/joomla/joomla-cms/releases/tag/5.4.3) was released on 17 February 2026.

## Issue [47238](https://github.com/joomla/joomla-cms/issues/47238) – Firefox 148.0 indefinite TinyMCE refresh

Firefox version 148, released on 24 February 2026, causes the TinyMCE editor to flicker and content to load endlessly, making it impossible to edit article content. See Bugzilla Bug [2019320](https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=2019320).

### Resolution

This issue will be resolved in Joomla 5.4.4 by PR [46889](https://github.com/joomla/joomla-cms/pull/46889).

### Workaround (Before Joomla 5.4.4)

1. Please use a different browser or a version of Firefox prior to 148.
