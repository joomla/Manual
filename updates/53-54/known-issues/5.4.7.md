Joomla 5.4.7 Known Issues
=========================

Last Updated: 8 July 2026

[Joomla 5.4.7](https://github.com/joomla/joomla-cms/releases/tag/5.4.7) was released on 7 July 2026.

## Issue [48058](https://github.com/joomla/joomla-cms/issues/48058) – Alternative layout in articles is not honoured

Pull Request [#47752](https://github.com/joomla/joomla-cms/pull/47752) introduced this regression.
The alternative layout selected for an article is no longer honoured.

### Resolution

This issue will be resolved in Joomla 5.4.8.

### Workaround (until Joomla 5.4.8)

Revert the changes introduced by PR [#47752](https://github.com/joomla/joomla-cms/pull/47752) manually,
at your own risk. Before making any changes, create a full backup and test the revert on a staging or test site first.
